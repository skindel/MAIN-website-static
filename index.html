Maastricht · AI & Innovation Network

Maastricht's home
for builders.

MAIN, Maastricht's AI & Innovation Network, is a student-run community at Maastricht University built for anyone curious about artificial intelligence and technology. We're the place where students from every faculty come together to actually do something with AI: Business, Law, Psychology, Science, you name it.

You don't need a technical background. You don't need to know how to code. You just need to be curious about where technology is going and want to be part of it.

We currently bring together 150+ registered members across all faculties, a WhatsApp community of 250+ students, and we collaborate directly with Maastricht University.

What we do

This is how we do it

Panel Talks

We bring industry experts, professors, founders, and researchers into the room to talk about what's actually happening in AI right now. Every panel includes a Q&A session where students drive the conversation — so it never just feels like a lecture.

Hands-on Workshops

Partners from industry and academia teach students how to use real AI tools and workflows. You leave with something you can immediately apply in your own projects, studies, or future career.

Hackathons & Build Nights

Companies and partners bring a real challenge, and students build creative solutions in a short amount of time. Real problems, real outcomes — and sometimes a product that actually ships.
